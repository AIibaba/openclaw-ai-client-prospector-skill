## Best Practices

1. **Research Before Reaching Out**: Spend 10 minutes per prospect researching recent news, funding, hiring, product launches
2. **Personalization At Scale**: Reference specific details in first line (company news, product feature, team member move)
3. **Lead with Value**: Show you understand their pain BEFORE asking for meeting
4. **Multi-Touch Persistence**: Most deals close after 5-7 touches; don't give up too early
5. **Track Everything**: Log all interactions in CRM to refine pipeline velocity
6. **A/B Test Subjects**: Test different subject line angles to optimize open rates
7. **Respect Boundaries**: If contacted, honor opt-out; reputation matters long-term
8. **Segment Aggressively**: Different industries, company sizes need different approaches
